Prominent Artists:

Narendra Singh Negi:

He has sung in every style of singing popular in Uttarakhand be it Jagar, Chaumasa, Thadya, or Playback. He has sung in different local languages like Garhwali, Jaunsari, Rawalti, etc. prevailing in the state. He started his music career by releasing "Garhwali Geetmala". These Garhwali Geetmalas came in 10 different parts. His first album came with the title called Burans. Burans is a well-known flower found on hills. He has released the most super-hit albums. He has also given his voice in many Garhwali movies like "Chakrachal", "Gharjawain", "Meri Ganga Holi Ta Maima Aali" etc. This renowned singer from Garhwal has sung more than 1000 songs till now.

Pritam Bhartwan:

Pritam Bhartwan is a folk singer from Uttarakhand, India. In 2019, he was conferred the Padma Shri honour by the president of India, Ram Nath Kovind, for his contribution to the field of traditional folk art. He also known as jagar Samrat in Uttarakhand. He has received many awards by State government and central government. He is also visiting professor in Cincinnati University and University of Illinois University.

Gajendra Rana:

Gajendra Rana (born 1980) is an Indian singer who is particularly notable in Uttarakhand state, India. His discography includes "Maalu", "Leela" "Ghasyari", "Rani Gorkhani", "Pushpa Chori" and "Hema Marchayani".

Meena Rana:

Meena Rana is an Uttarakhandi singer who sings primarily in Kumauni and Garhwali languages. She is considered to be one of the finest female singers of Uttarakhand. and is also the most recorded female singer in Uttarakhand. She is often referred to as 'Lata Mangeshkar of Uttarakhand' and 'Sur Kokila' of Uttarakhand.

Manglesh Dangwal:

Manglesh Dangwal was born on 5th June 1981, is one of the popular Garhwali singers from Uttarakhand who sings famous folk songs. Today many Uttarakhand folk lovers have become his fans because of his melodious voice and his songs. He has been born and brought up in Dehradun, Uttarakhand.
